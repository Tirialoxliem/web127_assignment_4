The Importance of Accessibility on the Web

The accessibility of websites is extremely important. Almost one in five people in the United States have some form of disability, that is 20% of the country. When you choose to not factor in accessibility issues that people can have while using your website, you can literally lose 20% of the business that you could have had if you chose to address these issues. A good reference for building a website while addressing accessibility issues for people with disabilities can be found at eChalk. As you navigate through my website you will learn common disabilities that people have that cause accessibility issues, and ways to confront these issues.
