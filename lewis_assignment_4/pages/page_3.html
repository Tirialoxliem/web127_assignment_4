The Importance of Accessibility to the Hearing Impaired

Just like with vision problems, there is an extreme amount of people in the United States with hearing impairments. If you do not consider the hearing impaired population of the users visiting your site, you could also lose a significant number of customers for your business. Although using a computer is a little less of a struggle for somebody that is hearing impaired, there are still some struggles that you need to address on your web site. There are two main items to address for someone with a hearing impairment. The first item to address is to make sure that all your videos have closed captions available, this way someone with a hearing impairment can still follow along with your videos. The second item would have to do with podcast, or similar audio items, you will want to have a transcript, so the users with hearing impairments can utilize your audio items by reading the text of them. I have included a very educational video to the left of this text that educates you about keeping hearing impaired children involved in the classroom by making sure they are able to hear the entire conversations between teachers and students during classroom time.
