The Importance of Accessibility to the Dexterity Impaired

Dexterity is a major impairment issue with a huge variance of severity. These types of impairments could be from an accident, from old age, from a disease that surfaces on someone, or even from just being born with it. It could be someone with shaky hands, no grip, no control of their hand muscles, or even someone without hands. These people cannot be left out when it comes to using computers, and the internet. One thing that you want to make sure is that someone is able to use keyboard functions to use your web site. This is because some people have a very difficult time using a mouse, so they will use functions on the keyboard like the arrow buttons to maneuver around the page, or the tab button to go left and right on the navigation bar to choose which page they would like to go on. You also want to use good headers, and good menu organization also to make it easier for the users to know where they need to go. The video I have here to the right of this text is a very touching story of this child, and how she uses a computer to be able to talk to people, because she is not able to speak herself. This shows the very importance of people of all disabilities needing to be able to utilize a computer properly, someone like this needs a computer to even communicate with someone.
